## Centering
If you can't find your chart (you may have scrolled it out of view) then simply press the Center button in the toolbar. This will center the chart and zoom to the maximum level to contain your entire chart.

![](.guides/img/center.png)

## Zooming
You can zoom

- by using the zoom buttons
- by using the mouse scroll wheel if you have one.

The zoom buttons are the 2 buttons in the above image that are not circled.
